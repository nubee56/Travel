Thailand

Most exotic places in Thailand

Phuke

The most popular tourist island in Asia, with white sand beaches,tropical mountain ranges and Buddhist temples such as Big Buddha Phuket and Wat Chalong.

Northern mountainous region

The cradle of Thai civilization, with cities such as Chiang Mai, the northern capital, and Chiang Rai, a rafting and trekking center.

Similan Islands

The 11 islands are located in the Andaman Sea, Phang Nga province, home to mountains of limestone covered with the lush green of tropical plants, virgin beaches with fine white sands and transparent turquoise waters Phi Phi Islands. Ko Phi Phi Don is the largest island and also the transport center. On the island there are many beautiful beaches such as Nui, Ton Sai and spectacular entrances: Phak Nam Bay, Wang Long Bay and Loh Ba Kao Bay.
